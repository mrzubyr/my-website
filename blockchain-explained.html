Blockchain Explained

Understand blockchain technology in simple terms.

Blockchain is the underlying technology behind cryptocurrencies. It is a decentralized digital ledger that records transactions across many computers in such a way that the records cannot be altered retroactively. This ensures trust and security without a central authority.

How blockchain works:

  • Transactions are grouped into blocks.
  • Each block is linked to the previous block, forming a chain.
  • The ledger is distributed across multiple nodes (computers).
  • Everyone in the network can verify transactions, making it transparent and secure.

Blockchain is used not only in crypto but also in finance, supply chain, healthcare, and many other industries for secure and transparent data management.
